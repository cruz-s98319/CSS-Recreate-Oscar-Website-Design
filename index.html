Oscar Care: An entire team dedicated to you

Oscare Care gives you personalized virtual care from the comfort of your home, at work, or on-the-go. Plus, with Oscar's Virtual Urgent Care and Virtual Primary Care1, you can book a visit with a provider for $0, anytime, anywhere.2 Every Oscar member is also paired with a dedicated Care Team to answer questions and guide you to the care you need at just the right time.
